## Data Use And Acknowledgements

This build uses 45 Bundibugyo ebolavirus records shared through [Pathoplexus](https://pathoplexus.org). Of these, 10 sequences were submitted directly to Pathoplexus and 35 were ingested into Pathoplexus from INSDC sources. All sequences used in this build are available through [Pathoplexus SeqSet PP_SS_2047](https://pathoplexus.org/seqsets/PP_SS_2047).

Some records are marked RESTRICTED and were shared pre-publication to support public health response. Please follow the [Pathoplexus data use terms](https://pathoplexus.org/about/terms-of-use/data-use-terms), including seeking permission from the data contributors before using restricted sequences in preprints or publications.

The analysis is generated from the [GenomicEpi fork](https://github.com/genomicepi/ebola) of the Nextstrain Ebola workflows. Additional outbreak context and sequence announcements are available on [virological.org](https://virological.org/t/initial-genomes-from-may-2026-bundibugyo-virus-disease-outbreak-in-the-democratic-republic-of-the-congo-and-uganda/1032).

Sequence data were generated by the Pathogen Genomics Laboratory at INRB in the Democratic Republic of the Congo, with leadership from the Ministry of Public Health, Hygiene and Prevention, INRB, INSP-COUSP, provincial health divisions, and outbreak response teams.
